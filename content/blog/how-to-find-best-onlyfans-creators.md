## Introduction

## Look at the Subscription Price

Creator subscription prices range from free up to $50/month or more. FansPedia's price filter lets you set a maximum budget so you only see creators within your range.

A good strategy is to start with free or low-cost creators in a category you're curious about, then upgrade to premium creators once you know what style you enjoy.

## Read the About Section

The "About" blurb on each creator's FansPedia profile card gives a quick idea of their content style and personality. Creators who write detailed, thoughtful bios tend to be more active and engaged with their audience.

## Check Bundle Deals

Many creators offer discounted bundle subscriptions ‚Äî for example, 3 months for the price of 2. FansPedia shows bundle info directly on creator cards. If you're planning to subscribe long-term, bundles can save you significantly.

## Conclusion

The best strategy combines category browsing, the verified filter, and a price range you're comfortable with. Start broad, find a few creators you enjoy, then use FansPedia's similar-creators feature to discover more like them.
